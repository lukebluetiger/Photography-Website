    About Me

    Hey I'm Luke, in my free time I love to take photos of nature and the outdoors when I'm traveling. Most of my photos try to capture the beauty of nature and it's importance in our world. Nature heavily influences most of my photography but I also enjoy taking photos of friends and places I travel to.

    I use a Sony a7 II for my photography and my main lens for landscapes is the GM 24mm f/1.4. I also am available for portraits. Feel free to contact me here leaving details of what you need.
